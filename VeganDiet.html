Affordable Vegan Diet - South of Brazil

Sample Affordable Vegan Diet Plan

This sample affordable vegan diet plan is tailored for the South of Brazil and aims to provide a budget-friendly and balanced vegan diet for your region.

Breakfast

1. Oatmeal with sliced bananas or locally available fruits

2. A glass of water or herbal tea

Lunch

1. Rice or whole grain pasta

2. Black beans (a cost-effective source of protein in Brazil)

3. Steamed or stir-fried mixed vegetables (using local and seasonal options)

4. A side salad with local greens

5. Local fruits for dessert (e.g., oranges, papaya, or apples)

Snack

1. Whole grain bread or crackers with guacamole or hummus

2. A piece of local fruit (in season)

Dinner

1. Sweet potatoes or yams

2. Vegan feijoada (a Brazilian black bean stew) with local spices

3. Roasted local vegetables

4. Side salad with local ingredients

5. A glass of water or herbal tea

Snack

1. Vegan yogurt with a drizzle of local honey or agave syrup (if available)

Notes:

  • This budget-friendly diet uses cost-effective ingredients available in the South of Brazil.
  • Black beans are a great source of affordable protein and a staple in Brazilian cuisine.
  • Use locally grown and seasonal fruits and vegetables to reduce costs and environmental impact.
  • Adjust portion sizes based on your dietary needs and budget.
  • Consider consulting a local nutritionist or dietitian for personalized recommendations.
