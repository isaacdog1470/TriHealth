Ketogenic Diet - South of Brazil

Sample Ketogenic Diet Plan

This sample ketogenic diet plan is tailored for the South of Brazil and aims to provide a low-carb, high-fat diet for your region.

Breakfast

1. Scrambled eggs with spinach and avocado

2. A glass of water or herbal tea

Lunch

1. Grilled chicken or fish with a side of mixed greens and olive oil dressing

2. Cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles

3. Local vegetables sauteed in olive oil

4. A small portion of local cheese

Snack

1. Greek yogurt with a sprinkle of chia seeds

2. A piece of local fruit (in moderation)

Dinner

1. Baked salmon with a side of asparagus and garlic butter

2. A mixed green salad with avocado and nuts

3. A glass of water or herbal tea

Snack

1. A handful of mixed nuts and seeds

Notes:

  • This ketogenic diet is low in carbohydrates and high in healthy fats.
  • Local vegetables and ingredients can be incorporated while maintaining the low-carb aspect of the diet.
  • Portion sizes should be adjusted based on individual dietary needs and goals.
  • Consult a healthcare professional before starting a ketogenic diet, as it may not be suitable for everyone.
